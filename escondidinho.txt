Ingredients:

4 - potatoes
500g - of meat
1 cup - milk
1/2 - onion
200g - cheese

procedure:

1- cook the potatoes
2- smash the cooked potatoes
3- add milk until it becomes a mash potato
4- add salt to taste
5- cook the meat
6- add one layer of mash potatoes in a retangular container
7- add a second layer of meat
8- cover all with a third layer of mash potatoes
9- add cheese to the top of it
10- take it to the oven to grill.

done
